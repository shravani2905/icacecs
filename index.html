About ICACECS 2024

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, VNR VJIET, has successfully organized the International Conference on Advances in Computer Engineering and Communication Systems (ICACECS) annually since 2020, in collaboration with Springer Nature International and Computer Society of India (CSI).

This year, ICACECS 2024 is scheduled for 8th & 9th August 2024, co-located at THE UNIVERSITY OF THE WEST INDIES (UWI), at Mona, Jamaica.

Co-Hosts

The University of the West Indies (UWI), Mona, Jamaica

The founding campus of the unique, multi-part, multi-national University of the West Indies, UWI Mona is the premier educational institution in the region, with extensive undergraduate, masters, and doctoral programs.

Mohan Babu University, Tirupati, India

Mohan Babu University promotes excellence across diverse fields, fostering talent for future-ready careers. The university emphasizes academic brilliance and prepares students to excel in companies like Google and Amazon.

About VNR VJIET

Established in 1995, Vallurupalli Nageswara Rao Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNRVJIET) is a premier institution known for academic and research excellence in Telangana.

VNRVJIET excels in:

  • Producing skilled engineers at undergraduate and postgraduate levels
  • Conducting groundbreaking fundamental research
  • Encouraging innovation, societal impact, and revenue generation through new products

Our Department - CSE

Founded in 1995, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at VNRVJIET focuses on advancing computing and its applications to build intellectual capital.

CSE offers programs in:

  • Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering
  • Bachelor's in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) with TCS
  • Master's in Software Engineering (SE)
  • Master's in Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Equipped with advanced labs and dedicated faculty, the department conducts pioneering research with a focus on global excellence and local relevance.

For more information, visit us at www.vnrvjiet.ac.in/cse.php
